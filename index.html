About Physics Stack Tower

Drop moving blocks to build the tallest tower possible! Perfect alignments give bonus points, while misaligned drops shrink blocks and make the challenge harder.

Features:

  • 7 levels with increasing difficulty
  • 3 difficulty modes (Easy, Medium, Hard)
  • Sound effects and visual feedback
  • Physics-based tower stability
  • Score tracking and progress saving

Controls: Click or press SPACE to drop blocks
